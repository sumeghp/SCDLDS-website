Workshop

Explorations in Statistics, Probability, Learning and Optimization Research

Date & Time 14th January 2025 to 18th January 2025
Venue Takshila | AC-02 | LR-007, Ashoka University
Organizer Centre for Data, Learning and Decision Sciences, Ashoka University
Organising Committee
  • Vivek Borkar (IIT Bombay)
  • Sandeep Juneja (Ashoka University)
  • Praneeth Netrapalli (Google DeepMind)
  • Sumegh P (Ashoka University)
  • KS Mallikarjuna Rao (IIT Bombay)

The Safexpress Centre for Data, Learning and Decision Sciences (CDLDS) at Ashoka University is delighted to announce exSPLOre 2025, a five-day workshop from January 14-18, 2025, exploring the evolving and exciting areas of quantitative research underpinning the developments in data science, machine learning and artificial intelligence. The workshop begins with two days of basic tutorials for graduates and advanced undergraduates, offering 3-hour sessions each on the basics of probability, learning, and optimization, led by leading experts. The following three days will comprise a research workshop, with talks by leaders in the field. The program includes a poster session for PhD students.
